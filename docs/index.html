Privacy Policy

Last updated July 4, 2026

Your location

When you grant permission, Sofra uses your device location to center the map. Your location is processed on-device only and is never stored, transmitted, or shared.

Your favorites

Restaurants you bookmark are saved locally on your device using Apple SwiftData. This data never leaves your device.

What we never do

No user accounts. No analytics. No advertising. No sale of data to third parties.

Restaurant data

The app loads restaurant names, addresses, and coordinates from Supabase. No personal information is included in these requests.
